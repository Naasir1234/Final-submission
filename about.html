About Flightigo Travels

Your trusted travel partner from Kannur, Kerala

Our Story

Founded in 2010 in the beautiful city of Kannur, Kerala, Flightigo Travels has grown from a small local agency to a respected name in the travel industry. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make world-class travel experiences accessible to everyone in our community.

Our Mission

We believe that travel has the power to transform lives. Our mission is to provide personalized, authentic travel experiences that go beyond the typical tourist routes. We carefully curate each itinerary to ensure our clients experience the true essence of each destination.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate travel experts with firsthand knowledge of the destinations we offer. Each member brings unique expertise, from luxury travel specialists to adventure travel gurus, ensuring we can cater to every type of traveler.
